🛡️ Cyber Security Tips

Simple Ways to Stay Safe Online

🔐 Strong Passwords

Use passwords with uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and symbols.

📱 Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA whenever possible for additional account security.

🚫 Avoid Suspicious Links

Never click links from unknown emails or messages.

🔄 Update Software

Keep your operating system and apps updated regularly.

📧 Beware of Phishing

Verify emails before sharing any personal information.

💾 Backup Important Data

Keep backups of important files to protect against data loss.
